About us

We specialize in advanced production deployments of large language models. We believe that the key to effectively utilizing AI in an organization is the precise configuration of models. We provide comprehensive support—from the initial idea, through model customization, to integration with corporate tools and maintaining the solution in the chosen environment.

We most often work with unstructured data derived from documents in various formats. Together with our clients, we design processes that enable the model to operate autonomously—without the need for constant supervision.

Our Collaboration with Senetic

We collaborate with Senetic on implementations within the Microsoft ecosystem. We create the best configurations for our clients that enable the reliable utilization of Azure AI capabilities. In turn, Senetic is responsible for managing areas related to compliance, hosting, and direct communication with the software manufacturer.
